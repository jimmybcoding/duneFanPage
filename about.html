About

Frank Herbert

The GOAT Author

Frank Herbert, born in 1920, was an American science fiction author best known for Dune (1965), a groundbreaking epic exploring politics, ecology, and religion. His intricate world-building and philosophical depth revolutionized the genre, making Dune a timeless classic and cultural phenomenon. To put it mildly, the intelligence of this man is mind blowing.

Denis Villeneuve

Director

Denis Villeneuve focused on making Dune a visually stunning and immersive film. He brought Arrakis to life with practical effects and detailed world-building. His passion for the story and dedication to its adaptation make him a standout director in modern sci-fi.
